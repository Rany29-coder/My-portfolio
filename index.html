Minerva Assignement :SS50: Complex Systems Analysis Case Study

This assignment was a transformative experience for me. It seamlessly bridged the theoretical concepts of linear algebra with practical applications, especially in the realm of image processing. The task allowed me to delve deep into the intricacies of matrices, vectors, and their interrelationships, making abstract ideas tangible. What I particularly cherished was the opportunity to view images not just as visual entities but as complex matrices filled with data, each pixel telling its own story. The challenge of representing grayscale images using vectors and understanding the nuances of orthogonal bases was intellectually stimulating. Moreover, the reflection process made me realize the profound impact of linear algebra in machine learning and its omnipresence in everyday technologies. This assignment was not just about solving problems; it was a journey of discovery, reinforcing my passion for the subject and its vast potential.
